Summary & Resources

Who is this for?

Communication instructors at RRC Polytech with varying AI experience.

Goal of the tutorial

Teach how to use ChatGPT to create in-class activities efficiently, safely, and effectively.

How it helps

  • Saves preparation time
  • Increases student engagement
  • Provides diverse activity ideas
  • Helps instructors adapt quickly

Scope

Covers: How to use ChatGPT, prompt writing, practical examples, ethical use, step-by-step demos.

Does not cover: Advanced automation, coding/plugins, AI grading of major assignments, policy-making.
