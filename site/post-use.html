Suggested Use of AI

What to use ChatGPT for

By Xiaojuan
  • Activity generation (main focus): warm-ups, prompts, scenarios, role-plays
  • Lesson warm-ups and writing prompts
  • Emails or announcements
  • Brainstorming class examples
  • Summaries of student writing (not grading)
  • Creating quiz questions

Do

  • Use AI for ideas and to save time
  • Adapt materials for different learners
  • Review and refine outputs

Don’t

  • Don’t use AI to grade final assignments
  • Don’t copy outputs directly without checking
  • Don’t rely on a single prompt—iterate
