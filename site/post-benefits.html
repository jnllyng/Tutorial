How AI is used + Benefits

Part A - How AI is used (workflow)

By Jueun & Md Akramul
  1. Open ChatGPT in your browser.
  2. Paste a clear prompt (e.g., “Create a 10-minute discussion activity on persuasive techniques for college learners”).
  3. Review the output; check tone, level, and time.
  4. Refine with follow-ups: “Shorter questions,” “Make it advanced,” “Add examples.”
  5. Copy the final version into your lesson plan.

Part B — Benefits to instructors & students

Instructors

  • Saves preparation time; reduces mental load
  • Generates variety fast; improves consistency
  • Supports lesson planning and alternative explanations

Students

  • More interactive, personalized activities
  • Exposure to current tech; rapid practice feedback
  • Diverse examples for different English levels

As noted by an AI specialist (Greve, 2025), creativity, prompt writing, and critical thinking are essential skills for future students.
