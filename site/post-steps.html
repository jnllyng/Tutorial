Example: Warm-up activity generator

  1. Goal: A 5-minute warm-up discussion on media bias.
  2. Prompt: “Create a 5-minute warm-up with 3 discussion questions about media bias for college Communication students.”
  3. Expected output: 3 concise questions plus timing.
  4. How to refine: “Make questions simpler,” “Aim for ESL B2,” “Add a short example headline.”
  5. Classroom use: Display questions on slides; run quick pair-share.

Example: Quiz-question generator

  1. Goal: 5 multiple-choice questions on persuasive appeals.
  2. Prompt: “Generate 5 multiple-choice questions on ethos, pathos, logos with answers, college level.”
  3. Expected output: 5 MCQs with keys.
  4. How to refine: “Shorter stems,” “Add one trickier distractor,” “Label each appeal.”
  5. Classroom use: Quick quiz or clicker questions.
